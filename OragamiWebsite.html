Intresting Facts About Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are a part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important of communication among Chameleons
  • Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around the branches

Intresting Facts about Camel!

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

Intresting Facts about Teddy-Bear!

  1. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
  2. In 1902 in Germany, Steiff launched “Bear 55 PB”, the first toy bear with jointed arms and legs.

Intresting Facts about Giant Panda!

  • A giant panda is much bigger than your teddy bear.
  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Intresting Facts about cicada!

  1. They prune mature trees, aerate the soil,
    and once they die, their bodies serve as an important source of nitrogen for growing trees.
  2. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  3. The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
  4. Most have red-orange eyes.
  5. Their short adult life is not unusual for bugs.
